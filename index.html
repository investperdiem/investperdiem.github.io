History

What was PerDiem?

PerDiem was a platform that allows anyone to invest in music. Artists could raise money by selling shares in their music and building a following of investors that literally have a vested interest in their success. Anyone could invest in music and earn money based on the artist's success.

We made it easy for anyone to invest in music. Never worry about contracts or legal fees. Artists could raise money by selling shares in their music projects. This gave people an incentive to fund the artists music as they could earn a return on their investment based on amount of sales.
